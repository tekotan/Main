GreenBall Platform Game on Scratch

Duration: August 30, 2018 - September 9, 2019
Partner: Abhishek Sarepaka Platform: Scratch
Objective: To build a scratch game or animation with user interaction and multiple stages.
Details: For the scratch project, we had to program a scratch game or animation that had user interation along with multiple stages. We chose to create a game because it appealed to our interests. We decided on creating a platform game with realistic gravity effects and movement that agrees with the laws of physics. Since I had previous programming experience, I took the lead in the design of the game, using my experience to teach my partner, while my partner handled the asthetic aspects of the project like designing the levels and characters of the game. Some challenges that arose was correctly modeling the projectile motion of the ball during a jump. We worked around this by researching the ways other people used in their own platform games and modeled our code after theirs. This project taught me how to divide and conquer tasks based on the skills available between the two of us.
